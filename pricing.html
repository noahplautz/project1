Before any project is started, a quote is given to the customer to verify the price. This is important so the customer and business are on the same page. Pricing all depends on how long the engine is being worked on. It also depends on how many parts will be needed to fix the engine and also the labor that is needed. We get a lot of our parts and oils from O'Reilly Auto Parts, NAPA Auto Parts, and Mills Fleet Farm.
